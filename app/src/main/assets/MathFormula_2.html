\(三角形重心\)

$$(1) \overline{AB} = \frac{2}{3} \overline{AD}；\overline{BG} = \frac{2}{3} \overline{BE}；\overline{CG} = \frac{2}{3} \overline{CF}$$

$$(2)\Delta {ABG} = \Delta{BCG} = \Delta{CAG} = \frac{1}{3}\Delta{ABC}$$

$$(3)\overline{AD}、\overline{BE}、\overline{CF}為\Delta{ABC}之三中線$$

$$\Rightarrow\Delta {AFG} = \Delta {BFG} = \Delta {BDG} = \Delta {CDG} = \Delta {CEG} = \Delta {AEG}$$
