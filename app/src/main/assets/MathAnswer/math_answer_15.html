\(f(x)=\sqrt{2x-1}=(2x-1)^{\frac{1}{2}}\)
\(f'(x)=\frac{1}{2}(2x-1)^{-\frac{1}{2}}\times 2 = (2x-1)^{-\frac{1}{2}}\)
\(f''(x) = -\frac{1}{2}(2x-1)^{-\frac{3}{2}}\times 2 = = (2x-1)^{-\frac{3}{2}}\)
\(\int_1^5 f''(x)dx = \int_1^5 -(2x-1)^{-\frac{3}{2}}dx = -(2x-1)^{-\frac{1}{2}}\times -2 \times \frac{1}{2}\mid_1^5\)
\(= (2x-1)^{-\frac{1}{2}}\mid_1^5 = 9^{-\frac{1}{2}} -1^{-\frac{1}{2}}=\frac{1}{3}-1 = -\frac{2}{3}\)
