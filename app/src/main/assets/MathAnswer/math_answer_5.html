\(\because\overline{AB}//\overline{CD}\) (已知)
\(\therefore \angle{ABO} = \angle{CDO}\)， \(\angle{BAO} = \angle{DCO}\) (同位角相等)
則 \(\Delta{ABO} ~ \Delta{CDO}\) (AA相似)
\(\overline{OA}:\overline{OC} = \overline{OB}:\overline{OD}\) (對應邊成比例)
\(\frac{12}{7}:\frac{10}{3} = \frac{18}{7}:\overline{OD}\)
\(\frac{12}{7}\overline{OD} = \frac{60}{7}\)
\(\overline{OD} = 5\)，則D點的座標為\((0, 5)\)
